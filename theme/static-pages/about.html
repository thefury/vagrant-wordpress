Design Applications

About Us

Design Applications provides architectural and design consulting services to people who want to enhance their urban residences and to retail/commercial enterprises that need competitively priced project management through some, or all of, the phases of planning, design and construction.

Design Applications can optimize the quality of your urban footprint by stretching and reshaping spaces using a variety of techniques. We build up, down and out, maximize natural light, and creatively use structural features—like bulkheads—to create hierarchy between rooms. Experienced with the older construction methods and features found in many Toronto homes, we find solutions that combine heritage or original elements with thoroughly modern design and materials in an aesthetic that is efficiently simple.

We also provide commercial/retail owner/operators with new construction and store alterations that pay special attention to efficient operations, product display and human-brand interaction. We’re distinguished by both our project management skills and our successful collaborations with trusted experts in engineering, landscape and interior design. Former clients have praised our consistent, careful management of each project to avoid costly errors and overruns. We understand fixed deadlines; our emphasis on reliability ensures on-time delivery.

Maureen Beckett, B.ES, B.Arch, OAA
Principal

Maureen Beckett is registered with the Ontario Association of Architects and brings over 20 years industry experience to your project. Since obtaining degrees in both Environmental Studies and Architecture, she has worked in Canada and the United Kingdom and been exposed to a broad range of architectural styles.

The founder of Design Applications, Maureen is especially interested in urban and aged buildings that require challenging structural and spatial solutions, as well as commercial new builds and alterations that require careful management to be on time and on budget.

Maureen is the recipient of several Broadcast Designers Awards for work with the CBC.

Maureen’s expertise:

  • Design and Working Drawings
  • Approvals Processes
  • Interior design and Space planning
  • Project Management
  • Site Supervision
  • Office Design
  • Industrial Design
