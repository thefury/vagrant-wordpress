Strathcona Avenue

Optimizing Footprint

Additions aren’t always necessary to upgrade a property or attain a sense of spaciousness. This complete two-storey renovation was accomplished with a small increase to the floor plate on the second storey that didn’t require special approvals—the result of expert knowledge of the building code and its provisions.

Modern finishes in kitchens and bathrooms, reconfiguration of space to add storage closets, and new extra-wide sliding glass doors in the rear of the house, open up, lighten and modernize the space.

A roof fin, anchored by chains, replaces a tired porch to multi-purpose effect; it supports a new bay window above and covers the home’s entryway while increasing light into the lower-level front rooms.
