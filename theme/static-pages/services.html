Services

We’re experts in the entire process—from design through construction—so you don’t have to be. It’s our job to know about zoning and codes (such as the Ontario Building Code) and how to apply for building permits and other approvals.

We collaborate with trusted experts in structural, civil, mechanical and electrical engineering. We build a team from our network of builders, manufacturers and landscape and interior designers—specific to your project—who communicate effectively and who share our exacting standards.

Specifically, we have the capacity to:


  • Measure, document and prepare drawings of existing structures
  • Oversee and monitor building construction (by landlord) prior to client/tenant takeover
  • Analyze and address issues of zoning, Ontario Building Code and other codes
  • Prepare schematics and final designs
  • Consult/engage specialized consultants (e.g. engineering, inspection)
  • Supply interior design services
  • Supply industrial design solutions
  • Prepare and submit necessary documentation for approvals such as Building Permits, Site Plan Approval, Committee of Adjustment (minor variances) and Heritage Review
  • Prepare construction documentation
  • Issue a project tender and assist with the selection of a contractor
  • Prepare and administer standard construction contracts
  • Provide project management
  • Supervise site
  • Review and provide follow-up to performance issues
